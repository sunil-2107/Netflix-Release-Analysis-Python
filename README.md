## Netflix Data Analysis Project

Netflix Movie & TV Shows release analysis using python.

----
## Project Overview

The objective of this project is to analyze Netflix content data to identify trends in movies and TV shows and provide business insights. The analysis focuses on understanding the types of content available on Netflix and identifying patterns that can help the company make better decisions about future content production.

----
## Business Problem

Netflix has a large collection of movies and TV shows available worldwide. The goal of this project is to analyze the dataset and generate insights that could help Netflix decide:

* What type of content to produce
* How content distribution differs across countries
* Trends in movies and TV shows over time
* How Netflix can grow its business in different regions

----
## Dataset Information

The dataset contains a list of movies and TV shows available on Netflix along with important details such as:

* **show_id** – Unique ID for every movie or TV show
* **type** – Movie or TV Show
* **title** – Title of the content
* **director** – Director of the movie/show
* **cast** – Actors involved
* **country** – Country of production
* **date_added** – Date the content was added to Netflix
* **release_year** – Original release year
* **rating** – Content rating
* **duration** – Duration of the movie or number of seasons
* **listed_in** – Genre/category
* **description** – Short summary of the content

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Analyze movies release pattern using countplot.
* Compare TVshows & movies through line plot & pie plot.
* Find out best time for launching TVshows by line plot.
* Analysis of actors/directors of different types of shows/movies using multiple bar plot.
* Analyze does netflix favour TVshows more through line plot.
* Understanding what content is available in different countries using split and value_counts function.


----
## Key Insights

Some important insights from the analysis include:
* Mostly increase in numbers of movie releases through the decades.
* Their was a certain rise in high number of movie releases rather than TV show.
* March,April,June,July,August & November is best month for release TV shows.
* Frequent collaborations usually indicate successful past projects.
* Movie release numbers are more than 2x from TV shows release number.
* These markets already have strong entertainment industries and high content demand.

----
## Key Recommendation

* Netflix should maintain a balanced strategy between movies and TV shows.
* Movies should still be produced, but TV Shows should remain a strategic focus for subscriber growth.
* Netflix should launch high-budget or flagship TV shows during peak viewing months, especially on month of September – December.
* Well-known actors and directors attract audiences and increase the chances of a show's success.
* TV Shows keep users engaged for longer periods because viewers watch multiple episodes across several days or weeks.
* Producing local-language content helps Netflix attract regional audiences and increase global subscriptions.
